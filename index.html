About FLYJIN

Flyjin is an avant-garde hospitality management, providing a choice of brands catering to all niche market segments.

We provide sophisticated luxury or urban elegance, a family retreat or a beach destination, a budget hotel, and all within the Flyjin umbrella. Every brand has its own unique personality and exceptional ambiance, which provide a distinct experience, so does every ambitious developer looking to cater to their clients. Focusing on anchor brands for developer that can be leveraged.

Our principle is to deliver and guarantee heart-warming elegant & chic hospitality with world-class management to all our guests and partners. The pillars for Flyjin growth are brand diversification, innovation, core values, respect for local cultures, commitment to excellence, and the quality-of-service delivery.

Product diversification and innovation, sound fundamental values, respect and sensitivity for local culture, commitment to excellence, quality service and expansion in key destinations are the cornerstones behind Flyjin.

Elite brand positioning: We position quality brands within your location, there is a science behind choosing brands and giving proper ratio between entertainment, f&b and retail.
